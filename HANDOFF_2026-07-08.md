## What changed

Both the ROPP dashboard's **TGL revenue** and the **CA close rate** now feed off the
dedicated **ESTIMATE AC/TGLS report** (canonical `ROPP_Estimate_TGLs.xlsx`, matched by the
phrase `"estimate ac"` in the filename) instead of the older "Scheduled vs Ran" report.

- **Revenue** — `sub_by_srcjob()` sums each sold estimate's *Estimate Sales Subtotal* per
  lead-source job number, joined back to the TGLs-Created report.
- **CA close rate** — new helper `tgl_estimates()` yields `(source_tech, scheduled_date, subtotal)`;
  `build_close_rate()` iterates it. Sold = `Subtotal > 0`; Ran = count of estimate rows keyed on
  the Scheduled/Ran date (= ServiceTitan Opportunity Date).
- **Columns are header-detected** (works with the report's flat layout), with an automatic
  **fallback to the Scheduled vs Ran report** if the ESTIMATE file is absent — the build never breaks.
